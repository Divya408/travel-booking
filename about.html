Our Story

Built by people who over-packed their carry-ons

Meridian began in 2011 as a shared document three friends used to track flight deals for each other. What started as a favor between travelers turned into a company because the spreadsheet kept getting shared outside the group chat.

Today the same instinct drives the product: every route on Meridian has been walked, flown, or sailed by someone on the team before it's listed. We don't sell places we haven't been.

Our Mission

Make booking a trip take less time than deciding what to pack — without cutting corners on the parts that actually matter, like a hotel that looks like its photos.

Our Vision

A world where "off the beaten path" isn't a marketing phrase — where every traveler can find a route as specific as their curiosity.
